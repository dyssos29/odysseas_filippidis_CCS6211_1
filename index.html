Climate Zones

Tropical

This climate is located near the equator. It is hot and humid year-round with frequent rainfall.

Temperate

This climate is located south of the arctic area at the North Pole and north of the arctic area at the South Pole. It has moderate temperatures with distinct seasons.

Subtropical

This climate is located in regions between the tropics and the temperate zones. It is a bit warmer than the temperate climate and has distinct wet and dry seasons.

Arctic

This climate is located at the poles. It has extremely cold temperatures with ice and snow most of the year. The ground there is frozen all year round, resulting in a phenomenon known as permafrost.
